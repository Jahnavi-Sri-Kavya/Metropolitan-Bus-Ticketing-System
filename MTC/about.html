ABOUT


This Ticket booking website was first introduced by Jahnavi, Naren and Prarthana, 2nd year Software engineering Students pursuing their degree at VIT Chennai. The main aim of this web portal was to maintain Social distancing and to avoid contact with the conductor and other passengers in the bus, as it promotes the spread of Corona virus. The idea of this web portal was proposed in December 2019 and was implemented in June 2020 amidst the COVID-19 pandemic and has earned huge applauds from the Public.
